Need home workout ideas?

Try out this simple app to generate a fun, at-home-friendly workout that's tailored to you!

You'll have your choice of three types of workout:

  • A circuit-style workout, where you'll complete 3-5 rounds of a seven-exercise sequence
  • A block-style workout, where you'll complete 3 rounds of an two-exercise "A-block" pairing, three rounds of a two-exercise "B-block" pairing, and three rounds of a three-exercise "C-block" tri-set
  • A freestyle workout, where you'll complete a total number of reps for seven exercises. The total reps can be broken up however you'd like, and completed in any order.

Want to try it out? Just hit the "run" button below and reply to the prompts that appear in the console. (Note that it may take a few seconds for the software to load.)

If you enjoy your workout, please share this app with others! If you have ideas for how this app can be improved, please fork the code and add your own updates, or let me know what you'd like to see changed.
